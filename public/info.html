Project Info

What is the Group Design Project?

The Group Design Project (GDP) is an integral part of the Aeronautical Engineering MEng course. Working in groups of about 25, students are given a brief and 5 weeks to deliver a project. They are aided by a panel of academic supervisors from the Aeronautics department whom they present to in a weekly debrief.

Autonomous Airship 2020

This GDP group was made up of 25 students split into 6 subteams. We worked together over 5 weeks to create an initial design from scratch. Take a look a the Project Timeline section below to find out more about our design process.
