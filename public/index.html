AIM Airship

Welcome!

This web app is art of the Autonomous Airship Group Design Project of 2020. Use it to interact with the airship by giving voice commands, using virtual joysticks, or request it to navigate to a specific location.

Click on the menu button on the top left to discover more.

What is AIM Airhsip?

AIM Airship, meaning Autonomous Interactive Mapping Airship, is the brainchild of a group of 25 third year students from the Aeronautical Engineering MEng programme.

Find out more in the Project Info page.
